# 🚀 Welcome to Cline Configuration 101

> **Your practical guide to configuring Cline — the AI coding agent — for maximum efficiency and minimum cost.**

---

## Who Is This For?

You're a **solo founder** or **independent developer**. You may not have a software engineering background. You want Cline to help you build faster, but you don't want to waste money on expensive API calls or hours configuring things you don't understand.

**This repository is your shortcut.** Every configuration is explained in plain English, copy-paste ready, and designed to save you money.

---

## The Learning Path (4 Levels)

| Level | Time Investment | Goal | Cost Focus |
|-------|----------------|------|------------|
| 🟢 **Level 1: Quick Start** | ~30 minutes | Get Cline working today | Use cheap/free models |
| 🟡 **Level 2: Efficient** | ~2 hours | Save time & money daily | Smart model selection |
| 🔴 **Level 3: Power User** | ~4 hours | Automate everything | Multi-model optimization |
| ⚫ **Level 4: Scale** | ~2 hours | Prepare for team growth | Enterprise-grade setup |
