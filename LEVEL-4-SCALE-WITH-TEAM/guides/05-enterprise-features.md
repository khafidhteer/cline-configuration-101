## What Are Enterprise Features?

Cline offers enterprise features for teams that need:
- **SSO** — Single sign-on with your company credentials
- **RBAC** — Role-based access control (Member, Admin, Owner)
- **Remote config** — Centralized settings for all team members
- **Prompt storage** — Backup conversations to S3/R2
- **OpenTelemetry** — Export metrics to Datadog/Grafana
- **MCP governance** — Control which MCP servers are allowed

---

## When You Need Enterprise

| If you're... | You probably don't need Enterprise |
|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| Solo founder | ✅ |
| Small team (2-5) | ✅ Git + shared rules is enough |
| Growing team (5-20) | Maybe — depends on compliance needs |
| Company (20+) | Likely — for SSO, RBAC, governance |
| Regulated industry | Likely — for audit trails, prompt storage |

---

## Roles

| Role | What They Can Do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Member** | Use Cline with shared providers |
| **Admin** | Configure providers, manage members |
| **Owner** | Billing, ownership transfer |
